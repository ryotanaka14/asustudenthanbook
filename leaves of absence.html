XIV. LEAVE OF ABSENCE

1. Prolonged leave of absence shall require a written permission to the Dean or equivalent official, stating the reasons for the leave and shall specify the period of the leave, which shall not exceed one academic year. The College/School/institute, through the Dean or his authorized representative, shall notify the parents or guardians of every student granted leave of absence and the Registrar for the amount of money to be refunded to the student, if any.

2. Students who withdraw from a College/School/Institute/Center without formal leave of absence, may have their registration during that semester curtailed or entirely withdrawn. A student who stops for one or more semester, but fails to apply for leave of absence, shall be required to reapply for admission.
