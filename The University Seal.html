The University Seal

1. The Book with Torch to symbolize knowledge and enlightenment which the university will impart along its different mandated programs.

2. The words Instruction, Research, Production, Extension which are the quadrology of functions where the university will primarily provide dynamic leadership.

3. The motto “Labor is Honor” inscribed on the book to dignify honest effort in working for development and helping the university’s clients become self-reliant and productive.

4. The year 1918 – the year when the university was founded as Capiz Farm School.

5. The circle of rope along which are written the words Aklan State University and Philippines representing the strong organizational bond among the different sectors founded on dynamism and adhocracy.

6. The dominant colors shall be green and gold which stand for progress and prosperity.
