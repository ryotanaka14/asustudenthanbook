Kalibo Campus

The ASU Kalibo Campus was the former Roxas Memorial School of Arts and Trades which came into existence by virtue of Republic Act 1592 as amended by R.A. 1417. The school formally opened on July 12, 1960.

On June 10, 1983, it became a National College the Roxas Memorial College of Arts and Trades through B.P. 471.

The campus which has an area of 8 hectares is situated along the busy Roxas Avenue Extension in Barangay Andagao, Kalibo, Aklan, and is now the ASU College of Industrial Technology (ASU-CIT)
