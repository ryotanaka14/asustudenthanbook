PART IV. SANCTIONS

Any member of the faculty, administrators or employees who violates the rules of confidentiality of the results of the drug test shall in addition to the sanctions provided in the university policy, be liable under Section 72 of RA 9165 and other applicable laws. (CMO No. 18 s2018 Section 19)

If in any case the student violates the confidentiality of the drug test results, he/she shall be subject to the provisions of penalties pursuant to RA 10137 Data Privacy Act of 2012. Refusal to undergo drug testing, he/she shall be subject to DDB BR No. 3 s2009 Section 6 H and CMO No. 18 s2018 Section 15.
