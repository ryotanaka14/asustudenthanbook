PART III. CONFIDENTIALITY CLAUSE

All members of Drug Testing Committee shall guarantee the strict confidentiality and integrity of the drug test results. All records must be treated with utmost privacy as provided for under the pertinent provisions of RA 9165, DDB BR No. 3 s.2009 and CMO No. 18 s.2018.

No members of the DFC, faculty, administrators, employees or student representatives shall publish or post results whether positive or negative.
